Recommended approach

How to get the most out of this

Use the tools in this order for the best results. The whole sequence takes about a week.

1
📚
Read through the Interactive Course
Start with the course for each chapter. Read the lessons, study the conversion tables and code blocks, and answer the mini quiz at the end of every lesson. Build your foundation before testing yourself.
2
🃏
Run Flashcards for each chapter
After each chapter in the course, open the Study App → Flashcards. Filter to just that chapter and flip through all the cards. Mark what you know and what you miss. Builds vocabulary before you start quizzing.
3
📝
Practice Quiz — chapter by chapter
Once you've done flashcards for a chapter, open Practice Quiz, filter to that chapter only, and work through Easy → Medium → Hard. Aim for 80%+ before moving on. Read every explanation carefully.
4
🔁
Spaced Repetition — daily, 10 minutes
Every single day, open the Study App → Spaced Repetition and work through your due cards. The system tracks what you miss and brings it back at the right time. The most proven study method — do not skip it.
5
📊
Check Weak Spots Dashboard weekly
Open the Weak Spots tab to see which chapters are red. Drill those chapters specifically using the Practice Quiz. Don't waste time on chapters where you're already scoring 80%+.
6
🎯
Exam Simulation — once a week
Treat it like the real thing. No notes, no hints, full 45 minutes. This trains your brain to perform under exam conditions and reveals any remaining gaps before the actual test.
7
📋
Cheat Sheet — the morning of the exam
Open the Cheat Sheet, press "Expand all", and scan all the ⚠️ Exam Traps. These cover the most common mistakes in the exam. A 30-minute scan that morning can save you multiple points.
